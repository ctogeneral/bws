// Frequently asked

Still wondering?

When does the next cohort start?

Cohort 01 opens for booking in June 2026. Waitlist members are notified 48 hours before the public.

Is this only for kids?

No. Salem mentors students, parents, working professionals, and career switchers. Tracks are tailored per builder.

What if I'm a complete beginner?

Perfect. The Beginner track starts from zero — Portfolio site, Birthday card, then your first interactive app.

Do I have to pay to join the waitlist?

Not at all. Joining is free. You only pay when you book an actual project once your slot opens.

How much do sessions cost?

Projects start around $30 for a single short build and go up to ~$250 for the full Advanced AI track. Waitlist members get $25 off when the cohort opens.

How are sessions delivered?

Live, 1‑on‑1 over Google Meet or Zoom. You share your screen and we build together — no pre‑recorded videos, no passive watching.

What do I need to get started?

A laptop, stable internet, and curiosity. No prior coding experience required for Beginner and most Intermediate tracks.

How long is each session?

Sessions run 60–90 minutes depending on the project. You'll always leave with something working that you can show off.

Can I pick the project I want to build?

Yes. Browse the project catalog on the main site and pick whatever excites you. We can also customize a project around an idea you already have.

What happens after I join the waitlist?

You'll get a confirmation email, then a heads‑up the moment the next cohort opens. No spam, no daily newsletters — just the things that matter.

Can I get a refund if it's not for me?

Yes. If after your first paid session you don't feel it's the right fit, you get a full refund — no questions asked.

Do you mentor outside Nigeria?

Absolutely. Sessions are fully remote and timezone‑friendly. Builders from anywhere are welcome.
